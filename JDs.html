JDs

Jobs are posted on the JDs page with descriptions and requirements.

The JDs page is where job listings are posted. On the main page, you can view the job title, start date, end date, and if its active(if so, it is visible on the page when "active" is checked, and has a start and end date). To search for a specific job, use the search bar at the top. To upload a new job listing, click the "Upload JD Req" button. For additional details about a job, click the side navigation bar on the right of the row and select "Edit." This will provide more information, such as the client, job description, and required qualifications. At the bottom, the "Find Matching Consultant" button allows you to search for consultants who meet the job criteria. You can also use the "Update" button to modify the job's end date.
