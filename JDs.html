JDs

The JDs page is where jobs are posted. On the main page you can see the job title, start date, end date, and if its active. You can click the "upload JD req" button to upload a new job. For more information, click the 3 dots on the right of the row and then click "edit". There you can see more information, like which client its from, the job description, and required education and experience. You can use the "find matching consultant" button at the bottom to search for consultants who fit the criteria. The "update" button can be used to change the end date of the job.
