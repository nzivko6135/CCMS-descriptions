Clients

Clients are companies that need jobs done. Triwave gives them qualified consultants to complete those jobs.

Clients are companies seeking individuals to fill various job roles. When a consultant is deemed suitable for a position, Triwave refers them to the client. To find a specific client, use the search bar at the top, or click the "New Client" button to add a new one. The page lists all client names, and by clicking the side navigation bar on the right of each row, you can access more detailed information about that client. This includes their location, sector (government or private), and contact details for key personnel.
