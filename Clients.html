Clients

Clients are companies that need people for whatever job they need done. If a consultant is qualified for a job, Triwave refers them to the client. You can search for clients using the search bar at the top, and add a new one by clicking the "new client" button. On the page is a list of the names of the clients, and if you click the 3 dots on the right of the row, you can see more information about that client. This includes the location, sector(government or private), and contact information for important people.
