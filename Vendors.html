Vendors

Vendors are other companies that supply Triwave with consultants.

Triwave Solutions sources its consultants through vendors. To find a vendor, use the search bar at the top of the page, or add a new vendor by clicking the "New Vendor" button. The page displays a list of all vendors. For more details about a specific vendor, click the side navigation bar on the right of the vendor's row, then select "Edit." This will reveal additional information, including the vendor’s location, sector (government or private), and, if you scroll down, the names and contact details of key personnel.
