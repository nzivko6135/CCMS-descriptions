Vendors

Vendors are other companies that supply Triwave with consultants.

Vendors are where Triwave Solutions gets consultants from. You can search for a vendor using the search bar at the top, and add a new vendor by clicking the "new vendor" button. The page has a list of all the names of the vendors. If you click the 3 dots on the right of the row and then click "edit", you can see more information about that vendor. This includes the location and sector(government or private) and if you scroll down you can find the names and contact information for important people.
